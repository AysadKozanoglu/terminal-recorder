Terminal Recorder

Terminal recorder allows you to record your bash session, and export it to html so then you can share it with your friends.

  • Record your bash session
  • Export it to HTML
  • Share it with your friends
  • Make tutorials
  • Allow others to play and pause
  • Allow them to copy and paste
